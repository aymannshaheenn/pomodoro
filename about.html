About Pomodoro+

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. These intervals are known as "Pomodoros."

How Pomodoro+ Helps

Pomodoro+ brings this classic technique to your fingertips. With our sleek timer, customizable session lengths, task management, and motivation boosters, you can stay focused and productive throughout your day.

Features

  • Start and customize Pomodoro sessions
  • Manage your tasks and goals
  • Track your progress and history
  • Stay motivated with curated quotes and images
